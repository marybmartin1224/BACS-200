The Clear Cell line, by Image, is formulated for acne prone skin. Consistent use of these products will ensure healing of current break outs, prevent future breakouts, and exfoliate the skin for optimal balance.

Combinations of salicylic and glycolic acids combat the bacteria that forms within the skin's pores that causes blackheads, pastuals, and cystic acne. The Clear Cell line offers two different types of face wash, one serum, medicated acne masque, a toner, an oil-free daily lotion, and medicated acne pads.
