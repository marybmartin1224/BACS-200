Ageless is the perfect word to describe this skincare line, by Image. In particualr, this line is suitable for aging skin types such as signs of unwanted wrinkles, sagging of the skin tissue, and hyperpigmentation.

Forms of retinol, glycolic and lactic acids are integrated into these formulas. The Agless line offers a face wash, serum, retinol, eye cream, and a total resurfacing masque. Overall, this line is more abrasive and intense, so slowly incorporating these products into your skincare regime is beneficial to allow the skin to adapt.
