The Salvation Army

Mission Statement: The Salvation Army, an international movement, is an evangelical part of the universal Christian Church. Its message is based on the Bible. Its ministry is motivated by the love of God. Its mission is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and to meet human needs in His name without discrimination.

Profile: About Salvation Army

The Salvation Army provides many different services for those in need. Those services include 1. Helping children 2. Disaster relief 3. Aiding adults to finding homes 4. Wokring with international issues.

Ways to give and support the Salvation Army include: donations of clothing and homegoods, become a fundraiser, and donate monthly to help with overhead costs.

The Salvation army's priority is to be a helping hand for those in need to escape from abuse, bullying, poverty, hunger, veterans, and spiritual healing.
